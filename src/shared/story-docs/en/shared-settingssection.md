## props

- `children`: The rows, `SettingRow` and `SettingButton`, in the order the design lists them.
- `title`: The group's name, rendered as an `h3` so the page stays navigable by heading.

## stories

- `Data And Backup`: The design's data section, row for row, including the destructive tone.
- `Statement Only`: A statement-only section, like the design's privacy block.
